# Code of Conduct

`uDOS-sonic` is committed to a respectful, constructive, and welcoming project
environment.

## Our Standard

We expect contributors and participants to:

- be respectful and professional
- help newcomers learn the repo safely
- give clear, constructive feedback
- keep discussion focused on the work

We do not accept:

- harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- abusive or insulting language
- deliberate disruption or bad-faith participation
- publishing private information without consent

## Scope

This applies to:

- issues
- pull requests
- discussions around this repository
- docs, wiki, and contribution surfaces maintained for Sonic

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions
that violate these standards.

Responses may include:

1. clarification or warning
2. temporary restriction
3. permanent removal from project participation

## Reporting

Report serious issues directly to the project maintainer through a private
channel where possible, with enough context to review the incident.

## Attribution

This file is informed by the Contributor Covenant community standard and adapted
for the smaller governance surface of `uDOS-sonic`.
